About Our Firm

Experienced Legal Representation in Port St. Lucie for Nearly 50 Years.

Honest Advice. Experienced Lawyers.

At Messer & Messer Law Offices, our attorneys provide clients throughout the Treasure Coast area with dependable and experienced legal representation. Our calm demeanor and honest advice keep people coming back.

Roger Messer has practiced law in the community for nearly 50 years, and his son, Steven Messer, is a lifelong resident with nearly 20 years of legal experience. We know how and when to utilize our local resources to solidify our clients' cases.

Putting People First

You can learn a lot about people by looking at the company they keep. At Messer & Messer Law Offices, we never represent insurance companies. We represent people who have been injured or have other important legal issues to resolve for their families.

We care about you and your needs. Our firm helps residents with legal problems ranging from serious injuries and criminal allegations to planning for their estate and legacy.
